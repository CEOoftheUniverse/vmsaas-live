Frequently Asked Questions

How does tracking work?
When someone clicks your link, a 30-day cookie is set. If they sign up within that window, you get credited. We also store the referral in localStorage for persistence.
When do I get paid?
Commissions are calculated monthly and paid out by the 15th of the following month via PayPal or Stripe transfer. Minimum payout is $25.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?
No limit. The more people you refer, the more you earn. Top affiliates earn $500+/mo.
Can I refer myself?
No. Self-referrals are automatically detected and voided. Referrals must be unique, genuine customers.
