AI for Email: Drafting, Triage, Summarization & Follow-Up Automation

Email is both the primary channel through which business gets done and the primary source of knowledge-worker burnout. AI doesn't eliminate email — it eliminates the cognitive overhead of managing it, so your attention goes where it creates value.

The average knowledge worker spends 3.1 hours per day on email — reading, triage, drafting, and status checking. AI agents compress this to under an hour by handling the mechanical layers while surfacing only what requires genuine human judgment.

Six AI email workflows

✍️

AI Email Drafting

Generates first-draft email responses using context from the thread, your communication style, and relevant knowledge base content — so you review and send rather than compose from blank. ↓70% email drafting time across high-volume inboxes. Adapts tone to recipient and context.

↓ 70% email drafting time
📥

Inbox Triage

Classifies and prioritizes incoming email by urgency, topic, and required action — separating time-sensitive items that need same-day response from FYI threads, newsletters, and items that can wait. Gives you a curated action list rather than a raw inbox to process.

Curated action list from raw inbox
📋

Thread Summarization

Summarizes long email threads — especially useful for rejoining a conversation after vacation or catching up on high-volume threads. Extracts decisions made, action items assigned, open questions, and current status without requiring you to read 47 replies sequentially.

Any thread → 60-second catch-up
🔁

Automated Follow-Up

Tracks emails expecting a response — flagging threads that haven't received a reply within a defined window and drafting follow-up messages ready for one-click send. Eliminates the common failure mode where important requests get buried and forgotten.

Zero dropped follow-ups
📊

Email Analytics

Analyzes inbox patterns — response time by sender, volume by topic, time-of-day distribution, and common request types — giving you the data to optimize your communication workflow and identify systemic issues causing email volume spikes.

Data-driven inbox optimization
🛡️

Smart Unsubscribe & Filtering

Identifies low-value email categories — newsletters you don't read, automated notifications, marketing sequences — and creates filtering rules to route them out of your primary inbox automatically, reducing inbox noise without requiring manual rule creation per sender.

↓ Inbox noise without manual rules
