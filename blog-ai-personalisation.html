AI for Personalisation: Customer Experience, Product Recommendations, Content, Pricing & Lifecycle

Personalisation is no longer a competitive differentiator — it is the baseline expectation that customers have when they engage with digital products and services. AI gives ecommerce companies, SaaS platforms, and digital brands the ability to personalise at the level of individual customer context across every touchpoint: product discovery, content experience, pricing, onboarding, and lifecycle communications — driving the conversion rate, average order value, and retention improvements that generic one-size-fits-all digital experiences cannot deliver.

The digital businesses outperforming on conversion rate, customer LTV, and NPS in 2026 are those going beyond segment-level personalisation to individual-level experiences that treat each customer as a cohort of one — and AI is the only mechanism that can deliver this at the scale digital businesses require.

Six AI personalisation workflows

✨

Customer Experience Personalisation

Personalises the customer experience across every digital touchpoint — homepage, search results, navigation, and UI configuration — based on individual browsing history, purchase patterns, segment membership, and real-time context. ↑35% session revenue per visitor and ↑28% add-to-cart rate from AI-personalised customer experience versus identical homepage experiences served to all visitors regardless of intent and context.

↑ 35% session revenue per visitor
🛍️

Product Recommendations

Generates personalised product recommendations — cross-sell, upsell, complementary, and replenishment suggestions timed to individual purchase patterns and lifecycle stage. ↑42% recommendation click-through rate and ↑22% average order value from AI-personalised product recommendations versus collaborative filtering-based "customers also bought" carousels that ignore individual taste profiles.

↑ 42% recommendation click-through rate
📰

Content Personalisation

Personalises content feeds, email newsletters, push notifications, and in-app content surfaces — surfacing articles, features, and messages that match each user's engagement history and stated preferences. ↑55% content engagement rate and ↑30% session depth from AI-personalised content experience versus editorial curator-selected content served uniformly across the entire user base.

↑ 55% content engagement rate
💲

Dynamic Pricing

Personalises pricing — promotional offers, bundle pricing, loyalty pricing, and timing-based discounts — at the individual customer level based on purchase history, price sensitivity signals, and competitive context. ↑15% gross margin per transaction and ↓28% discount cost per acquired customer from AI dynamic pricing versus blanket promotional discount campaigns deployed to all customer segments simultaneously.

↑ 15% gross margin per transaction
🚀

Onboarding Personalisation

Personalises the user onboarding experience — adapting tutorial paths, feature introductions, and early-stage activation flows to the specific use case, role, and technical sophistication of each new user. ↑45% onboarding completion rate and ↓30% time-to-first-value from AI-personalised onboarding versus static linear tutorial sequences that lose users who arrive with different contexts.

↑ 45% onboarding completion rate
📧

Lifecycle Communications

Personalises lifecycle communications — timing, channel, message, and offer — based on individual customer signals including engagement recency, purchase behaviour, churn risk score, and support history. ↑48% email click-through rate and ↓35% churn rate triggered by timely, relevant lifecycle interventions from AI-personalised communications versus generic monthly newsletters.

↓ 35% churn rate
