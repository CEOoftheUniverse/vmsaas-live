AI for Accounts Receivable: Collections Automation, Credit Management, Cash Application, Dispute Resolution & AR Analytics

Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) is one of the most direct measures of working capital efficiency — and for most B2B businesses, reducing DSO by 5-10 days generates more free cash flow improvement than multiple years of incremental revenue growth. AI gives finance teams, credit controllers, and AR departments the ability to automate collections outreach with the personalisation that drives payment without damaging customer relationships, assess credit risk with data that traditional credit scoring misses, apply cash automatically at the transaction level, resolve disputes with the speed customers require, and generate AR analytics that connect receivables performance to working capital and customer health outcomes.

The companies achieving DSO reductions of 15-25% while simultaneously improving customer satisfaction with their billing experience in 2026 are those using AI to replace the blunt-instrument collections approach — phone calls at the highest-balance accounts, uniform dunning letters at fixed intervals — with a precisely timed, customer-contextually-aware collections strategy that maximises payment probability at minimum relationship cost.

Six AI accounts receivable workflows

📞

Collections Automation

Automates collections outreach — identifying the optimal timing, channel, and message for each customer based on payment history, invoice age, customer segment, and relationship value. ↓22% DSO and ↑35% collection rate from AI-optimised collections versus uniform dunning schedules that apply the same approach to customers with fundamentally different payment behaviour profiles.

↓ 22% DSO
📊

Credit Management

Manages credit risk dynamically — evaluating creditworthiness from financial data, payment behaviour, industry signals, and alternative data sources; setting and adjusting credit limits; and alerting teams to deteriorating customer credit profiles before invoices become overdue. ↓40% bad debt write-off rate and ↑28% credit decision accuracy from AI credit management versus static credit scoring based on historical financials alone.

↓ 40% bad debt write-off rate
💳

Cash Application

Applies customer payments automatically — matching remittance advice, bank statement entries, and partial payments to the correct invoices using AI pattern recognition, achieving straight-through application for the 85-95% of payments that match cleanly. ↑88% straight-through cash application rate and ↓70% cash application time from AI cash application versus manual remittance matching that creates AR reconciliation backlogs.

↑ 88% straight-through application
⚖️

Dispute Resolution

Resolves invoice disputes with speed — classifying dispute reasons, routing to the correct internal resolver, tracking resolution timelines, and generating the documentation required for credit memo or re-invoicing workflows. ↓55% dispute resolution cycle time and ↑30% dispute resolution rate from AI dispute management versus manual dispute tracking in email threads.

↓ 55% dispute resolution time
📬

Dunning Optimisation

Optimises dunning strategies by customer segment — personalising the sequence, tone, and timing of payment reminders to the customer's payment pattern, relationship tier, and dispute history. ↑25% dunning response rate and ↓15% DSO from AI-personalised dunning versus one-size-fits-all dunning schedules with fixed intervals.

↑ 25% dunning response rate
📈

AR Analytics

Generates AR performance analytics — DSO trend by segment, aging analysis, collection effectiveness index, bad debt forecasting, and working capital impact modelling. ↑55% AR analytics coverage and ↓40% AR reporting time from AI AR analytics versus manual AR KPI reporting.

↑ 55% AR analytics coverage
