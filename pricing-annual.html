Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel my annual plan early?

Yes. You can cancel at any time from the customer portal. If you cancel within the first 30 days, you receive a full refund. After 30 days, your plan continues until the end of your annual billing period.

Can I upgrade mid-year?

Yes. Upgrades are prorated. If you upgrade from Base to Swarm 6 months into your annual plan, you pay the prorated difference for the remaining 6 months.

Is the price locked in?

Yes. Once you subscribe, your price is locked for the full 12-month term even if we raise prices. On renewal, the new pricing applies.

Do I get all the same features as monthly?

Yes — same features, same GPU tier, same model access. The only difference is 20% off the price and a 12-month billing cycle.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Annual plans can also be paid by wire transfer — contact us for an invoice.
