AI Document Processing: Extraction, Classification & Intelligent Routing

Documents are the primary input to most business processes — invoices, contracts, forms, reports, claims. Manual document processing is the single largest source of operational headcount in knowledge-work businesses. AI eliminates it.

Traditional OCR solved the character recognition problem but not the understanding problem. Modern AI document processing understands document structure, extracts fields semantically, classifies by type and intent, and routes intelligently — handling the 30% of edge cases that break rule-based systems.

Six AI document processing workflows

🔍

Intelligent Data Extraction

Extracts structured data from semi-structured and unstructured documents — line items, totals, dates, party names, terms, and custom fields — with 95%+ accuracy across document variants without template programming. Handles PDFs, scanned images, and digital-native formats.

95%+ extraction accuracy, no templates
🏷️

Document Classification

Classifies incoming documents by type, priority, and routing destination — separating invoices from purchase orders, flagging urgent items, and routing to the right processing queue automatically. ↓85% manual document sorting time across high-volume intake operations.

↓ 85% manual document sorting time
📋

Form Processing

Processes structured forms — applications, registrations, surveys, and compliance forms — extracting all field values, identifying incomplete submissions, and populating downstream systems automatically. Handles handwritten fields via AI recognition.

↓ 90% form processing time
✅

Validation & Exception Routing

Validates extracted data against business rules, reference data, and cross-document consistency — auto-approving clean documents and routing exception cases to human reviewers with AI-generated context explaining the specific issue for efficient resolution.

Straight-through processing for clean docs
🔄

Multi-Format Handling

Processes documents regardless of format — native PDFs, scanned paper, email attachments, photo captures, and Word documents — with a single processing pipeline that normalizes format differences before extraction. Eliminates format-specific workflow silos.

One pipeline for all formats
📜

Audit Trail Generation

Creates complete, timestamped processing records for every document — what was extracted, who reviewed it, what decisions were made, and what exceptions were flagged — providing the audit trail required for compliance, dispute resolution, and process improvement.

Complete audit trail per document
