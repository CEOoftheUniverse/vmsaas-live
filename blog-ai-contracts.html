AI for Contract Management: Drafting, Review, Risk Extraction & Obligation Tracking

The average enterprise has thousands of active contracts and no systematic way to know what's in them. AI changes this — making contract knowledge searchable, obligations trackable, risks visible, and drafting dramatically faster for every team that touches contracts.

Contract management failures aren't legal problems — they're information problems. Obligations missed because no one was tracking them. Risks accepted because no one read the indemnification clause. Renewals auto-renewed at wrong rates because no one flagged the date. AI solves the information layer.

Six AI contract management workflows

✍️

Contract Drafting

Generates first-draft contracts from template libraries and deal parameters — NDAs, MSAs, SOWs, licensing agreements, and custom contract types — delivering a complete starting point that legal counsel reviews and refines rather than drafting from scratch. ↓70% initial drafting time per contract type.

↓ 70% initial contract drafting time
🔍

Clause Extraction

Extracts and structures key clauses from any contract — liability limits, payment terms, termination rights, IP ownership, non-compete provisions, and governing law — making contract terms searchable and comparable across your entire contract portfolio without manual review.

Full portfolio clause visibility
⚠️

Risk Scoring

Scores contracts against your risk policy — flagging unusual liability exposure, one-sided indemnification, missing limitations of liability, problematic IP assignments, and non-standard governing law provisions — prioritizing legal review on the contracts that actually need it.

Prioritized legal review on high-risk contracts
📋

Obligation Tracking

Extracts and tracks contractual obligations across your portfolio — deliverable deadlines, reporting requirements, certification maintenance, and notice periods — generating obligation calendars that prevent the missed deadlines that constitute breach without requiring manual tracking per contract.

Zero missed contractual obligations
🔔

Renewal & Expiration Alerts

Monitors contract renewal dates, notice windows, and auto-renewal deadlines — alerting responsible parties with enough lead time to make informed decisions rather than discovering auto-renewals after they've already triggered. ↑Negotiation leverage when renewals are managed proactively.

Proactive renewal management
⚖️

Counterparty Comparison

Compares counterparty paper against your standard positions — identifying deviations clause-by-clause, flagging unacceptable terms, and generating redline summaries that help legal counsel focus review time on the actual points of difference rather than reading every clause fresh.

↓ 60% contract review time
