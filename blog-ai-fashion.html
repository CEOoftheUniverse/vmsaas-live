AI for Fashion: Trend Forecasting, Demand Planning, Visual Merchandising, Sustainability & Personalisation

Fashion moves at a speed that makes every buying and design decision a bet on future consumer preference — and the penalty for getting it wrong is markdown margin erosion or stockout-driven lost sales. AI gives fashion brands, retailers, and fashion tech companies the ability to forecast trends earlier than competitors, plan buys with the granularity that reduces both excess inventory and missed demand, merchandise products visually at scale, track sustainability credentials, and personalise the discovery experience for every shopper.

The fashion brands outperforming on sell-through rate, markdown depth, and customer acquisition cost in 2026 are those using AI to compress the gap between trend emergence and buying decision — and to personalise the customer experience across every digital and physical touchpoint.

Six AI fashion workflows

🔮

Trend Forecasting

Identifies emerging fashion trends from social media imagery, runway shows, street style, and search data — forecasting which silhouettes, colours, fabrics, and aesthetics will drive demand before they peak. ↑30% new season sell-through rate and ↓22% markdown depth from AI trend-informed buying versus retrospective trend analysis from industry publications that lag trend emergence by 6-12 months.

↑ 30% new season sell-through rate
📊

Demand Planning

Plans buy quantities at style, colour, and size level — incorporating trend forecasts, customer demand signals, store transfer efficiency, and markdown sensitivity to optimise the opening buy that determines season profitability. ↓35% end-of-season excess inventory and ↓20% stockout rate from AI demand planning versus historical sales extrapolation that misses trend-driven demand shifts.

↓ 35% end-of-season excess inventory
🖼️

Visual Merchandising

Automates product photography enhancement, outfit styling recommendations, and catalogue visual composition — reducing the production cost of visual content while improving the editorial quality that drives conversion in digital channels. ↓50% visual merchandising production cost and ↑25% product page conversion rate from AI-enhanced visual merchandising versus manual styling and photography workflows.

↓ 50% visual merchandising production cost
📐

Size Intelligence

Analyses customer body measurement data and historical return patterns to generate size recommendations that reduce return rates and improve fit satisfaction — the primary driver of repeat purchase in apparel. ↓38% size-related return rate and ↑28% repeat purchase rate from AI size intelligence versus generic size guides that generate the fit uncertainty that drives excessive multi-size ordering and return rates.

↓ 38% size-related return rate
🌿

Sustainability Tracking

Tracks supplier environmental credentials, material carbon footprint, chemical compliance, and circular economy metrics — generating the verified sustainability data that increasingly determines brand positioning and regulatory compliance. Powers the transparent scope 3 emissions reporting that institutional investors and regulators require from fashion businesses.

Verified sustainability data pipeline
✨

Personalisation

Personalises product discovery for each shopper — surfacing styles that match their aesthetic preferences, fit history, and browsing behaviour across digital and email channels. ↑45% email click-through rate and ↑32% revenue per session from AI-personalised fashion discovery versus bestseller-led product recommendation carousels that ignore individual style profiles.

↑ 45% email click-through rate
