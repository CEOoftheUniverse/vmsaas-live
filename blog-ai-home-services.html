The home services operations achieving the highest revenue per technician in their market in 2026 are those using AI to ensure no enquiry waits more than minutes for a response, no scheduling gap goes unfilled when a cancellation occurs, and every completed job is followed up with the maintenance plan conversation and review request that compounds customer lifetime value.
